Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-18

Summary

  • GPX files are processed locally in your browser by default.
  • We do not upload GPX files to our servers.
  • Map tiles are requested directly from third‑party map providers.
  • Optional feedback messages are sent to our email provider.

Data We Process

  • GPX files: parsed locally in your browser for visualization and export.
  • Usage preferences: stored in local storage (e.g., UI state, feedback prompts).
  • Feedback submissions: if you send feedback, we process your message and optional email address to reply.

Third‑Party Services

  • Map tile providers: Esri (World Imagery/Wayback), OpenStreetMap, OpenTopoMap, Carto, OpenSnowMap, USGS, and OpenTopography. Your browser requests tiles directly from these services, which may receive your IP address.
  • Email delivery: Feedback is sent via Resend.

Your Choices

  • You can use TrailReplay without uploading any files.
  • You can avoid submitting feedback if you prefer not to share contact info.
  • You can clear local storage in your browser to reset preferences.

Contact

  • If you have questions, reach out via the feedback form or GitHub issues.
